About Our School

Ranked as one of Egypt's premier STEM institutions

Our Journey

Founded in 2024 in the heart of New Cairo, New Cairo STEM School (NCSS) began with a bold vision: originally designed to unite Egypt’s brightest STEM students into one hub of innovation. Though later reshaped as a public STEM high school, its spirit of excellence, ambition, and discovery remain at its core.

Our Foundation

With support from USAID, World Learning, and the Egyptian Ministry of Education, NCSS is part of a growing national STEM network. Each year, it enrolls 130 students (65 girls and 65 boys) through a highly competitive admission process. With our second cohort arriving this year, NCSS is already building its legacy as a home for Egypt’s future pioneers.

Our Vision

NCSS is more than buildings and labs. As one of Egypt’s newest STEM schools, our students are the first generation the foundation of a story still being written. They are innovators, problem-solvers, and leaders in the making. The journey has only just begun. But the message is clear: The future starts here.
